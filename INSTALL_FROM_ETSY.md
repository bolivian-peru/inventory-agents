## Step-by-Step Installation

### Step 1: Find the App

- Open your Etsy Shop Manager
- Go to **Settings** → **Apps**
- Search for **"Inventory for Agents"**
- Click **"Learn More"**

### Step 2: Install

- Click **"Install App"**
- Review the permissions requested:
  - Read your shop information
  - Read your product listings
  - Send messages on your behalf (for customer support)

### Step 3: Authorize

- Click **"Allow"** to grant permissions
- You'll be redirected to the IFA dashboard

### Step 4: Configure (Optional)

- Customize your agent's personality
- Connect messaging channels (WhatsApp/Telegram)
- Set business hours (or leave 24/7)

## What Permissions Does IFA Need?

IFA requests these permissions to function:

- **Read Shop Info**: To know your shop name and settings
- **Read Listings**: To answer questions about your products
- **Read Orders**: To provide order status updates (optional)

**We never:**
- Modify your listings without permission
- Make changes to your shop settings
- Share your data with third parties

## After Installation

### Dashboard Access

After installation, you'll have access to:
- **Agent Status**: See if your agent is active
- **Message History**: Review conversations
- **Analytics**: Track response rates and performance
- **Settings**: Customize behavior

### Connecting Messaging Channels

To respond to customer messages, connect at least one channel:

**WhatsApp:**
1. Go to Dashboard → **Messaging**
2. Click **"Connect WhatsApp"**
3. Scan the QR code with your phone
4. Done!

**Telegram:**
1. Go to Dashboard → **Messaging**
2. Click **"Connect Telegram"**
3. Follow the bot link
4. Send `/start` to activate
